Cultural Heritage

  • Confucian Influence: Emphasis on education, respect for elders, and strong family values
  • Buddhist Practices: Influences daily life with principles of compassion and mindfulness
  • Traditional Arts: Water puppetry, folk music (ca trù, quan họ), and calligraphy

Cultural Facts

Culinary Heritage: Regional cuisines influenced by geography and history

Traditional Dress: The áo dài symbolizes elegance and national identity

Language: Vietnamese with 6 tones and regional dialects

Social Values: Harmony, respect, and community solidarity

Vibrant Traditions & Festivals

Tết (Lunar New Year)

The most important celebration with family reunions, special foods, and traditions to welcome good fortune.

Lim Festival

Famous for "quan họ" folk singing performances and traditional games in northern Vietnam.

Traditional Performance

Fan dance representing grace and traditional storytelling.
