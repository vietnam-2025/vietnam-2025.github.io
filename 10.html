Practical Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting Vietnam

Essential Information

Visa Requirements

E-visas available for citizens of all countries - apply online

Up to 90-day duration with multiple entry options

Passport must have 6 months validity from entry date

E-visa: $25 (single) / $50 (multiple) Processing time: 3-5 business days Apply: evisa.gov.vn

Best Time to Visit

North
Oct-Apr (Dry & Cool)
Central
Jan-Aug (Varied)
South
Dec-Apr (Dry Season)

Health & Safety

Vaccinations

Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, and routine vaccines recommended

Water Safety

Drink bottled or purified water; avoid ice in rural areas

Mosquito Protection

Use repellent; dengue fever risk exists year-round

Travel Insurance

Highly recommended; cover medical evacuation

Transportation Options

Motorbike/Scooter
Popular & flexible, license required
$5-10/day
Grab/Taxi
Safe & convenient in major cities
$1-5 per ride
Trains
Scenic routes, overnight options
$20-50 long distance
Sleeper Bus
Budget-friendly long distance travel
$10-30 long distance

Quick Tips:

Currency: Vietnamese Đồng (VND). ATMs widely available in cities. $1 ≈ 23,000 VND

Electricity: 220V, primarily Type A (two flat pins) and Type C (two round pins) sockets

Internet: Widely available. Local SIM cards affordable (~$5 for 30 days with data)
