Vietnam History Timeline

c. 2879 BCE – 938 CE

Ancient Era: Legendary Hong Bang period & Dong Son culture followed by 1,000+ years of Chinese rule resisted by the Trung Sisters.

938 CE

Battle of Bach Dang: Ngo Quyen defeats the Southern Han fleet, ending direct Chinese control.

939 – 1858

Dynastic Period: Ngo, Dinh, Ly, Tran, Later Le & Tay Son dynasties.
• Ly & Tran (1009–1400): Golden age; repelled Mongols.
• Later Le (1428–1788): Longest dynasty.
• Tay Son (late 18th C): Brief reunification.

1802 – 1945

Nguyen & Colonization:
• 1802: Nguyen Dynasty begins.
• 1858–1887: French conquest forms Indochina.
• Early 20th C: Nationalism rises; Ho Chi Minh.

1945 – 1975

Wars & Division:
• 1945: DRV proclaimed.
• 1946–1954: First Indochina War.
• 1954: Geneva split.
• 1955–1975: Vietnam War.

1975 – Present

Reunification & Modern:
• 1975: Fall of Saigon.
• 1986: Doi Moi reforms.
• 1995: ASEAN membership.
• Today: Rapid growth.
