Photography Tips:

Best light at Ha Long Bay is during sunrise or sunset when the karsts cast dramatic shadows

Visit Sapa rice terraces in September-October when they turn golden during harvest season

Bring a waterproof camera for cave exploration in Phong Nha-Ke Bang

Ninh Binh is most photogenic after light rain when the limestone reflects in the water
